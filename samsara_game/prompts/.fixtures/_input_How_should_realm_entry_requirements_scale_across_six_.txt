Realm entry requirements should scale progressively harder as the player moves toward the Triple Gem. Stats diminish while in the Hell realm (drain rate: -0.1/sec). The outer void boundary drains even faster. Ash is the only NPC with zero requirements — positioned at the edge near the void as the only escape path.

Progressive realm thresholds:

Outer realms (easy requirements):
- Hungry Ghost: Wisdom 5, Merit 5, Karma 5 — almost no barrier, just escape Hell
- Animal: Wisdom 20, Merit 15, Karma 15 — requires a few good choices

Middle realms (moderate requirements):
- Human: Wisdom 50, Merit 35, Karma 30 — requires meaningful progress
- Asura: Wisdom 90, Merit 65, Karma 55 — requires helping multiple NPCs

Inner realms (hard requirements):
- Deva: Wisdom 140, Merit 100, Karma 85 — near-complete ethical development
- Temple: Wisdom 200, Merit 150, Karma 120 — enlightenment threshold

Each barrier increases significantly from the previous. When a player hits a barrier they see exactly what stats they need, so they know to find more NPCs rather than wandering confused.

The difficulty increases scale roughly proportionally, so no single realm feels like an impossible jump. The progression mirrors the Buddhist teaching that the path becomes more demanding as wisdom deepens.
