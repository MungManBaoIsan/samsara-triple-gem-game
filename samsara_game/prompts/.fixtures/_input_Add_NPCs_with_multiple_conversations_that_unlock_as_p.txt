Here is the NPC system design with multiple dialogue stages unlocked by wisdom requirements.

NPCs are visible on the map as coloured shapes. Players walk up to them and press E to interact.

State machine: Each NPC tracks four states — initial, helped_once, transformed, enlightened. The state determines which dialogue tree is active.

Wisdom-based unlocking: Later dialogue stages have requirements. Example: Kara's second conversation requires wisdom >= 30; her third requires wisdom >= 60. Players must grow in wisdom before returning for deeper teaching.

NPC types:
- Suffering NPCs: Characters trapped in their realm's delusion (Kara in Hell, Maya in Hungry Ghost). Player helps them see clearly. These transform across conversations.
- Teacher NPCs: Elder Song in the Human realm teaches the Four Noble Truths and Eightfold Path. Wisdom requirements gate advanced teachings.

Content is stored in npcs.json so dialogue can be edited without touching code. Each NPC entry has name, realm, position, type, and a list of dialogue objects with their requirements and consequences.

Both suffering and teacher types are included across the six realms.
