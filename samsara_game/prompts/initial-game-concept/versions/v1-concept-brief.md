# v1 — Concept Brief (2025-11-28)

The original game pitch. Open-ended and theme-first — describes the world and its suffering, names the Triple Gem as the goal, and leaves all mechanics unspecified.

```
I would like if possible, to build a 2D game where the player is born into
samsara (a world of suffering). The aim of the game is to navigate your way
through this world full of difficulties such as being bullied, rejected,
losing loved ones, alcoholism, drug abuse, prostitution etc. People are full
of ignorance, delusion, hatred, greed, envy and other defilements. The world
is full of evil. The only place that is safe & peaceful is the triple gem at
the sacred temple. Eventually, the player who reaches the triple gem (ultimate
refuge) first wins.
```

**What this version got right:** Communicates the Buddhist theme clearly and sets the emotional tone. The contrast between the suffering world and the peaceful Triple Gem gives Claude the core narrative tension to work with.

**What was missing:** No age range, no educational purpose stated, no stance on depicting vs glorifying suffering. Multiplayer framing ("first wins") was later replaced with single-player.
